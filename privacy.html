Ordinary Archive Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2026.

Ordinary Archive is designed as a private, local-first multimedia journal. By default, your journal entries, media, transcripts, tags, places, weather context, settings, and generated Connections insights stay on your device. Optional cloud sync uploads an encrypted archive bundle to a cloud service you choose.

What You Create

You may create text, audio, video, photo, tag, place, weather, and interview response content. This content is used to show your journal, organize your archive, support optional local Connections insights, and provide export/delete controls.

Local Storage

On Android, journal metadata is stored in a Room database protected by SQLCipher, and media files are encrypted locally where feasible. App settings use Android Keystore-backed encrypted preferences. On iOS, journal files are stored with file protection and should be backed by platform-secure storage in production builds.

Permissions

Ordinary Archive requests permissions only when needed:

  • Microphone to record audio journal entries.
  • Camera to capture video or photo journal entries.
  • Photos to let you choose images or videos to add.
  • Location to attach optional place context.
  • Notifications to send gentle reminders you control.
  • Speech recognition to create optional transcripts where supported.
  • Calendar for optional future day-reconstruction context.

Connections

Connections are optional observations generated from your own journal entries to help you revisit themes, memories, and patterns. By default, this analysis happens on your device. You can turn it off, delete insights, or exclude entries at any time.

AI Processing

No cloud journal processing is enabled in this build. If cloud processing is added later, Ordinary Archive will ask for explicit consent before sending journal content off device and will explain what is sent, who processes it, why it is sent, retention, training defaults, and how to disable it.

Optional Cloud Storage Sync

If you enable cloud sync, Ordinary Archive creates an encrypted archive bundle on your device before upload. Microsoft OneDrive, Google Drive, Apple iCloud/CloudKit, or an Amazon-backed storage endpoint only receive the encrypted archive file. Your archive passphrase is not uploaded by Ordinary Archive.

Exports and Deletion

You can export entries, import encrypted archives, import media files, sync encrypted archive bundles, and delete journal data. Location metadata is excluded from standard exports by default. Deleting all data removes local journal metadata and locally managed media files.

Health and Safety Disclaimer

Ordinary Archive is for personal journaling, memory, and reflection. It is not medical advice, therapy, diagnosis, treatment, or crisis support.
